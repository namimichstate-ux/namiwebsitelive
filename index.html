Our Mission

Support

We provide a safe space for students to share their experiences and find a community that understands the challenges of college life.

Education

We aim to educate the campus community about mental health conditions, warning signs, and resources to reduce stigma and foster understanding.

Advocacy

We advocate for better mental health policies and accessibility on campus, ensuring every Spartan has the resources they need to thrive.

Who We Are

Ending the Silence at MSU

NAMI On Campus - MSU is a student-led organization that tackles mental health issues head-on. We are a mix of students who support loved ones who struggle with mental health , have battled mental health conditions and students who just want to make the world a better place.

College can be tough, but you don't have to navigate it alone. Through bi-weekly meetings, fun social events, and awareness campaigns we are building a network of care right here in East Lansing.
